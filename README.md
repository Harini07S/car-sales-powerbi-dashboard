**Car Sales Performance Dashboard**

Power BI | Data Analysis | Business Insights

📌 Overview

In this project, I built an interactive Car Sales Dashboard using Power BI to understand how a car dealership is performing across sales, pricing, and volume.

The idea was to take raw sales data and turn it into something useful—so that trends, patterns, and key business drivers become easy to identify.

🧠 Business Context

The dataset represents a car dealership selling multiple vehicle models across different regions.

As the business grows, it becomes difficult to track performance manually or identify what’s working well and what isn’t. A dashboard helps bring everything together in one place and makes analysis much easier.

❗ Problem

There isn’t a single, clear view of sales performance across time, products, and regions.

Because of this:

Trends are not easy to track
High-performing segments are not clearly visible
Decisions are not fully supported by data

🎯 Objective

The goal of this project was to build a dynamic Power BI dashboard that helps:

Track key sales metrics
Understand performance trends over time
Identify top-performing products and regions
Support better, data-driven decisions

📊 Key Metrics

Sales Performance

YTD (Year-to-Date) Sales
MTD (Month-to-Date) Sales
YoY (Year-over-Year) Growth

Pricing

Average Price (YTD & MTD)
YoY Change in Price

Volume

Cars Sold (YTD & MTD)
YoY Growth in Units Sold

📈 Dashboard Highlights

Sales Trend: Weekly sales trend to track performance over time
Product Analysis: Sales split by body style and color
Regional Insights: Sales distribution across regions
Company Performance: Comparison across different brands
Detailed View: Transaction-level data for deeper analysis

📈 Key Insights

Sales show an overall upward trend, with certain weeks performing better than others
A small number of companies contribute a large portion of total revenue
SUVs and Sedans appear to be the most popular body styles
Some regions consistently perform better than others
Average prices remain fairly stable over time
Overall sales volume has increased compared to the previous period

💡 Business Recommendations

Focus on high-performing segments like SUVs and Sedans
Strengthen presence in top-performing regions
Investigate underperforming regions for potential issues
Run promotions during slower periods to improve consistency
Explore pricing strategies to improve margins
Use the dashboard regularly to monitor trends and performance
